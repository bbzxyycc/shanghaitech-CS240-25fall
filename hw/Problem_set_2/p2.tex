\problem{}
Xiaoxiong encounters $n$ monsters sequentially, numbered from $1$ to $n$. Monster $i$ has health $a_i$ and requires $p_i$ stamina to defeat. Xiaoxiong starts with an initial stamina of $P$.

For each monster, Xiaoxiong has two choices:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Let the monster go} -- gain $i$ experience points, and \textbf{no stamina is consumed}.
    \item \textbf{Defeat the monster} -- consumes $p_i$ stamina, and gains
    \[
        \text{experience} = a_i + (x \bmod 10) \cdot a_i
    \]
    where $x$ is the total number of monsters defeated so far (including the current monster).
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Note:} If Xiaoxiong's remaining stamina is less than $p_i$, she \textbf{cannot defeat the monster} and can only let it go.

\textbf{Goal:} Determine the maximum total experience Xiaoxiong can earn.

\newpage
\noindent \textbf{Solution}:

\noindent \textbf{State Definition} Define $OPT(i,p,k)$ as 
the maximum experience gained when the remaining stamina is $p$ after dealing with the first $i$ monsters, and the number of defeated monsters modulo 10 is $k$.

Here, $i \in [0,n],p \in [0,P], k \in [0,9]$

\noindent \textbf{State Transition Equation}
\begin{itemize}
   \item Boundary conditions: Initially encountered no monsters. 
   Defeated monsters modulo 10 = 0. Experience is 0. $OPT(0,p,k)=0$. Other states are unreachable.$OPT(0,p,k)=-\infty,i=0$ but $k \neq 0$
   \item Case 1: 
   
   When defeating monster $i$ requires stamina $p_i$ exceeding Xiaoxiong's remaining stamina ($p_i > p$), monster i must be let go, and $OPT(i, p,k) = OPT(i-1, p,k) + i$.   
   \item Case 2: 
   
    Defeating monster $i$ requires stamina $p_i \leq$  Xiaoxiong's remaining stamina. You may choose whether to let monster $i$ go:     
    \begin{itemize}
        \item let monster i go: $OPT(i, p,k) = OPT(i-1, p,k) + i$
        \item defeat the monster: Xiaoxiong can get
        $ a_i + a_i * k$ experience,
        
        $OPT(i, p,k) =OPT(i-1,p-p_i,(k-1) \mod 10)+ a_i + a_i * k$
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}

Thus, State transition equation is
\newline

\noindent $OPT(i,p,k)=$
\begin{equation*}
    \left\{\begin{matrix}
 0&i=0 ,k = 0\\
 -\infty&i=0  \text{ but } k \neq 0\\
 OPT(i-1, p,k) + i& p_i>p\\
 \max\{OPT(i-1, p,k) + i,OPT(i-1,p-p_i,(k-1) \mod 10)+ a_i + a_i * k \}&p_i\leq p
\end{matrix}\right.
\end{equation*}
Thus, the maximum total experience Xiaoxiong can earn is $\max_{k \in [0,9]} OPT(n,P,k)$.
